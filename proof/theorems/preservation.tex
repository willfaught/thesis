\begin{theorem}
\label{pn}
\onehalfspacing
If $\Gamma\vdash_{HMS}e_{HMS}^{1}:T$ and $e_{HMS}^{1}\rightarrow e_{HMS}^{2}$ then $\Gamma\vdash_{HMS}e_{HMS}^{2}:T$.
\begin{proof}
By cases on the reduction $e_{HMS}^{1}\rightarrow e_{HMS}^{2}$.  Scheme reductions that do not contain Haskell or ML terms on both sides are omitted because $\Gamma\vdash_{S}e_{S}^{1}:TST$ and $\Gamma\vdash_{S}e_{S}^{2}:TST$ for any $e_{S}^{1}\rightarrow e_{S}^{2}$.
