\begin{theorem}
\label{pn}
\onehalfspacing
If $\Gamma\vdash_{HMS}e_{HMS}^{1}:T$ and $e_{HMS}^{1}\rightarrow e_{HMS}^{2}$ then $\Gamma\vdash_{HMS}e_{HMS}^{2}:T$.
